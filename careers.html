WHY CASPIAN

Built for tradespeople
who care how it's installed.

We're a small, growing crew with the kind of pipeline that keeps techs busy year-round and the kind of culture that lets apprentices actually learn the trade.

01 — STEADY PIPELINE

Year-round work.

A varied mix of builder rough-ins, residential renovations, and 24/7 service calls means our techs aren't seasonal — and aren't bored.

  • Builder partnerships across the GTA
  • Residential renos & retrofits
  • Service rotation (opt-in)
  • Multi-unit and light commercial
02 — TOOLS & TRUCKS

Premium tools & vans.

Stocked vans, Milwaukee tools, ProPress, hydro-jet rigs, leak detection gear. You get what you need to do the job right — no improvising.

  • New & well-maintained service vans
  • Full tool inventory on every truck
  • Modern diagnostics gear
  • Tool allowance for personal kit
03 — REAL MENTORSHIP

Mentorship for apprentices.

Apprentices work under licensed journeymen who actually teach. We pay for in-school terms, sponsor your ticket, and back you to finish your hours.

  • Paid school terms (309A track)
  • Hours signed, tracked, submitted
  • Cross-trade exposure
  • Clear path to journeyman
04 — CRAFT-FIRST CULTURE

Crews that respect the trade.

No corner-cutting, no shouting matches, no race-to-the-bottom bidding. We win work on quality — which means we hire and back people who do quality work.

  • Quality-first install standards
  • Foremen who back their crew
  • Direct line to ownership
  • Honest feedback, both ways
