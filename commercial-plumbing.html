WHAT WE HANDLE

The full commercial plumbing scope.

Restaurants on Queen West, retail strips in the core, condo portfolios in North York and Etobicoke — Caspian handles the whole stack of commercial plumbing work, on a contract or on a single call.

Restaurant + food service

Grease trap pump-outs and replacements, hood-side gas and drain runs, commercial fixture installs, and the kitchen drain work that keeps health inspectors happy. We schedule after close.

Retail + office plumbing

Preventive service for washrooms, kitchenettes, and break rooms — plus same-day emergency response for leaks, clogs, and fixture failures that close a storefront or empty a floor.

Multi-unit residential

In-suite plumbing (kitchens, baths, in-suite hot water) plus common-area work — risers, mechanical rooms, garbage rooms, parking-level drains. Tenant-friendly scheduling and access.

Property management contracts

Monthly preventive visits combined with on-call response on a single agreement. Predictable invoicing, one point of contact, and a real plumber on site — not a dispatch desk.

Commercial backflow + cross-connection

Annual backflow preventer testing, installation, and repair by certified testers. Cross-connection surveys and reports filed with the City of Toronto as required.

Sewer + storm drain maintenance

Hydro-jetting for grease and root intrusion, camera inspections of laterals, and storm-drain clearing. Scheduled preventive runs keep parking lots and mechanical rooms dry.

INDUSTRIES WE SERVE

Built for businesses with downtime cost.

Queen West, Ossington, Danforth, Yonge corridor.

Grease trap service, commercial hot water, dishwasher hookups, kitchen drains, mop sinks, and pre-rinse stations. We know the inspectors, we know the equipment, and we schedule the disruptive work after last call so service isn't interrupted.

02 — Retail + office buildings

Preventive contracts, after-hours service.

Quarterly preventive checks on washroom and employee facilities, fast-response service for leaks and clogs during business hours, and after-hours work for anything that requires shutting down a riser. Documented for landlord and tenant alike.

03 — Property managers

Residential + commercial portfolios.

Single point of contact — one PM at Caspian, one cell number, never a call-center handoff. Documented visits, predictable monthly invoicing for contract clients, and consistent crews so the building knows the plumber and the plumber knows the building.

04 — Hospitality

Hotels, cafés, bars — silent overnight service.

Hospitality clients can't afford guests hearing a wet-vac at 9pm. We run an overnight crew for hotels and busy hospitality venues — repairs, drain work, and fixture swaps completed between last guest and breakfast service. No business disruption.

FOR PROPERTY MANAGERS

What property managers actually need.

After a decade of working with Toronto property managers, the same four things come up on every initial call. We built our commercial service around them.

01 — Contact

Single point of contact

One PM at Caspian, one cell number. Texts answered, calls returned same day, no call-center triage. The person who quoted the work is the person you talk to when there's a problem.

02 — Records

Documented service records

PDF service report with photos, findings, and parts used — filed within 48 hours of every visit. The format your owner expects and your auditor will accept. Exportable to your CMMS.

03 — Invoicing

Predictable monthly invoicing

Contract clients get a flat monthly invoice for scheduled work, with itemised add-ons for emergency calls. No surprise after-hours surcharges on contract accounts — the rate is the rate.

04 — Schedule

After-hours, no disruption

Overnight scheduling on request for retail, office, hospitality, and tenant-occupied residential. We bring quiet equipment, work behind closed doors, and the morning crew never knows we were there.
