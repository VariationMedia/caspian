WHILE YOU WAIT

What to do before we arrive.

Four steps that take less than two minutes and can save you thousands in water damage. Do these in order while you're on the phone with us.

01 / SHUT OFF

Shut off main water

Find the main valve — usually in the basement near the front wall — and turn it clockwise until it stops.

02 / DRAIN

Open faucets to drain

Open the lowest faucet in the house and a few upper ones to release pressure and drain remaining water from the lines.

03 / POWER

Stop electrical near water

If water is near outlets, fixtures, or the panel, kill that circuit at the breaker. Don't step into standing water near live electrical.

04 / DOCUMENT

Document with photos

Take wide and close-up photos and videos of the damage before cleanup. Your insurer will need them, and we'll use them too.
