WHAT WE INSTALL

Every fixture in the house.

From a single faucet swap to a full bathroom renovation, Caspian installs plumbing fixtures across every room — sized correctly, sealed properly, and pressure-tested before we leave.

Kitchen faucets & sinks

Pull-down, pot-filler, bar, and prep faucets. Undermount, drop-in, and farmhouse sinks — with new supply lines and proper drain alignment.

Bathroom faucets & vanities

Single-hole, widespread, and wall-mount faucets. Full vanity swaps with new shut-offs, P-traps, and silicone seals. Levelled, plumb, finished.

Toilets & bidets

Standard, comfort-height, wall-hung, and smart toilets. TOTO Washlet and bidet seat installs with proper electrical and supply coordination.

Tubs, showers & frameless glass

Freestanding tubs, alcove installs, walk-in showers, and rough-in for frameless glass. Thermostatic valves, body sprays, rain heads — all balanced.

Dishwashers & ice makers

Dishwasher hookups with proper air gaps and braided supply lines. Fridge water line installs for ice and filtered water — with new shut-offs.

Garburators & instant hot water

InSinkErator garburators and Quooker-style instant hot taps. Wired, switched, and plumbed properly — no leaks, no tripped breakers.

BRANDS WE WORK WITH

Premium fixtures installed right.

We install every major brand — but premium fixtures only hold up if they're installed the way the manufacturer intends. Here are the lines we touch most often across GTA homes.

Kohler

Full-spectrum: kitchen, bath, toilets, and shower systems. We're factory-familiar with their Rite-Temp and DTV valves.

TOTO

Neorest and Washlet installs done with the electrical, supply, and venting coordinated correctly the first time.

Hansgrohe

Grohe

SmartControl and Rapido SmartBox systems. We carry the cartridges and trim plates needed for clean finishing.

Riobel

Canadian-made, designer-favourite. We handle the full Riobel range including Pro thermostatic and rough-in valves.

High-spec finishes that demand careful handling. We install with the manufacturer's torque specs and approved sealants.

Delta

MultiChoice rough-ins and Touch2O electronic faucets. Familiar troubleshooting on every solenoid and diverter we touch.

WHAT WE DON'T DO

We won't cut corners.

There's a reason fixture installs go wrong six months later — shortcuts on day one. Here's what Caspian refuses to do, even when the customer asks for it.

  • We won't install over rotted subfloor — we fix the cause first, so the new fixture isn't sitting on tomorrow's leak.
  • We won't reuse old supply lines on premium fixtures. Braided stainless lines go in fresh, every time.
  • We won't install fixtures that arrive damaged from the box — we document it for your supplier instead of hiding it under chrome.
  • We won't skip the water-pressure test before sealing walls. If something's going to leak, it leaks on our time, not yours.
